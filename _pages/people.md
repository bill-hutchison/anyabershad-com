## Join the Lab

We welcome inquiries from prospective **postdoctoral researchers**, **graduate students**, and **research assistants**.

If you are interested in joining the lab, please send a brief statement of interest and your CV to [abershad@mednet.ucla.edu](mailto:abershad@mednet.ucla.edu).

We are also always looking for motivated **undergraduate research assistants** interested in gaining experience in human behavioral pharmacology research.
