## Participate in Our Research

The Social Psychopharmacology Lab conducts controlled studies of psychoactive drugs in healthy adult volunteers and clinical populations. Our research is approved by the UCLA Institutional Review Board and conducted under strict safety protocols.

---

## Current Studies

### MDMA and Social Motivation in Schizophrenia
We are investigating the safety and tolerability of MDMA in individuals with schizophrenia spectrum disorder, as part of the first-ever clinical trial of MDMA in this population. This study is funded by the NIH Director's Early Independence Award.

**Who can participate:**
- Adults aged 18-55
- Diagnosis of schizophrenia spectrum disorder
- Currently stable on antipsychotic medication

---

### Studies in Healthy Volunteers
We periodically recruit healthy adult volunteers for studies examining how psychoactive drugs affect mood, social behavior, and stress responses.

**General eligibility:**
- Adults aged 18-45
- No history of psychiatric illness
- No current use of psychiatric medications
